About AHP

At AHP, everything begins with three things I care deeply about: design, data, and fun.


I picked up a guitar in my teens, fell head over cleats for cycling later on, and haven’t stopped tinkering since. ACP launched in 2023 to bring a bit of cheeky charm to cyclists’ gift boxes. AGP followed in 2024, giving guitarists their own slice of the fun.


Every product starts with a simple interrogation (of myself):

  • Does it make sense to someone who’s not inside my head?
  • Is it beautifully built—design-wise and materials-wise? This is giftware, not landfill.
  • And most of all, how will it land with the person unwrapping it? Will it spark joy and hold their attention once the novelty’s worn off?

That’s the aim. To make things people actually want to keep. Not just because they look good but because they feel right.


Gifts with a bit of soul, a lot of thought, and no tat.
