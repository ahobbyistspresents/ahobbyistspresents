Wholesale FAQs

Do you have a minimum order?

Yes, but we keep MOQs low so independents can test lines. MOQs vary by range—ask for the current list when you enquire.

What margins can I expect?

We provide wholesale price, recommended RRP, and any promo guidance on the trade list to support healthy indie margins.

Where do you ship from?

All orders ship from the UK. We’ll confirm lead time and shipping costs when you share your order volume and postcode.

Can you supply distributors?

Yes. If you’re a distributor, please include your territory and preferred onboarding terms in your enquiry.

Do you offer POS assets?

We can share imagery and copy for online listings and provide light-touch POS suggestions for in-store displays.
